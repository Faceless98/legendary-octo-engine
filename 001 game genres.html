Top 6 game genres



1) Action games:
Action games are currently the most popular video game genre. Including first person shooter titles like Halo and Destiny 2
and rouglikes like Enter The Gungeon or Nova Drift. Action games test your reflexes, hand-eye coordination and reaction time
delivering a punch of adrenaline to the player.

2) Sports games:
Sports games take up the second spot of this list and have been around since the start of gaming culture. Sports games
simulate traditional physical sports while the opponent team is controlled by an AI (artificial inteligence) or bots. Some
games are based on actual sport tactics and rules, and others underline the strategy behind the sport. Some noteable games
of this genre are FIFA, WII sports, and Mario & Sonic Olympic Games.

3) Adventure games: Adventure games hold the third place on this list and retain their audience with a fun new world and storyline to explore.
They retain their audience with a vast storyline with fun and exciting challenges and visuals. Some noteable examples are
The Ledgend Of Zelda, Metroid, Hollow Knight, and Sea Of Theives.

4) Battle Royale games: It was inevitable. Battle royale games take up the 4th position and while being one of the newest, took the world by storm
with titles like Fortnite,PlayerUnknown's BattleGrounds (PubG), Call of duty (COD) warzone, and Totally Accurate BattleGrounds (TABG).
Battle Royales have a massive earning rate, with Fortnite earning 1 billion USD a year and PubG earning 700 million USD in 2017

5) Role playing games: At number 5 we have Role playing games. Role playing games (RPGs) allow players to immerse themselves into rich fantasy worlds.
Massively multiplayer online (MMO) RPGs like World Of Warcraft, EVE Online and the upcoming Ashes Of Creation, and more story driven
RPGs like Dungeons and Dragons, Crash Bandicoot, and Rayman Ledgends.
